About

Ethan's Scuffed Games is an unblocked games website made to provide fun to students who can't access game websites due to blocks. It's currently pretty bare-bones but if your desperate it's not that bad.

This website has technically been in development April 2022, but the html adaptation has only been around since Febuary 2023. It was originally called Ethan's Cool Funnies but was renamed on the 31st of August 2022.

This website was originaly built on google sites using my school email. I decided to move the website here to reduce risk of my school finding out, and to make it available to people outside my school (my school has a private domain).
